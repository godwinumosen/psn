{% block content_title %}Who We Are{% endblock %}

The Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN), Rivers State Branch, is the umbrella body representing all registered pharmacists practicing within Rivers State. As a branch of the national body, we operate under the constitution of the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria while addressing the unique needs and challenges of pharmaceutical practice in our state.

Our Mission

To promote and maintain the highest standards of pharmaceutical practice in Rivers State and ensure optimal pharmaceutical care.

Our Vision

To be the foremost professional body advancing pharmaceutical excellence and innovation in Rivers State.
