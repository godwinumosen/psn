About PSN Rivers

The Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN), Rivers State Branch, is the umbrella body representing all registered pharmacists practicing within Rivers State.

Our Mission

To promote and maintain the highest standards of pharmaceutical practice in Rivers State and ensure optimal pharmaceutical care.

Our Vision

To be the foremost professional body advancing pharmaceutical excellence and innovation in Rivers State.
