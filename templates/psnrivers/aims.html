The Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria, Rivers State Branch, is dedicated to achieving the following aims and objectives:

1

To unite all pharmacists in Rivers State for the advancement of the pharmacy profession

2

To maintain the highest ethical standards in pharmaceutical practice

3

To promote the welfare of members and protect their professional interests

4

To collaborate with government and regulatory bodies on pharmaceutical matters

5

To encourage continuous professional development and lifelong learning

6

To promote research and innovation in pharmaceutical sciences

7

To enhance public awareness of the pharmacist's role in healthcare

8

To foster relationships with national and international pharmacy organizations

9

To advocate for policies that improve pharmaceutical care delivery

10

To support initiatives that improve access to quality medicines
