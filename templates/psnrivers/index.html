About PSN Rivers

Championing Excellence in Pharmaceutical Practice

The Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN), Rivers State Branch, is the representative body for all pharmacists practicing in Rivers State. We are committed to promoting the highest standards of pharmaceutical practice, ensuring the welfare of our members, and advocating for policies that protect public health

Established over four decades ago, our branch has grown to become one of the most vibrant chapters of the PSN, driving initiatives that impact both the profession and the communities we serve.
