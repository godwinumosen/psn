Key Provisions

Article 1: Name and Headquarters

The organization shall be known as the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria, Rivers State Branch, with headquarters in Port Harcourt.

Article 2: Membership

Membership is open to all pharmacists registered with the Pharmacists Council of Nigeria practicing or residing in Rivers State.

Article 3: Governance

The Branch shall be governed by an Executive Council elected by members at the Annual General Meeting for a tenure of two years.
