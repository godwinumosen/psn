Our Mission

At the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria - Young Pharmacists Group (PSN-YPG), we are committed to empowering Young Pharmacists to become leaders and catalysts for positive change in the profession and society. Through collaboration, innovation, and professional excellence, we equip individuals to drive impact at local, national, and global levels, ensuring sustainable development and progress in healthcare.

Our Vision

1. We envision a future where Young Pharmacists play a pivotal role in advancing the nation’s health, leveraging their expertise to improve patient care and public health outcomes.
2. We are dedicated to ensuring that Pharmacy remains a cornerstone of healthcare, continuously evolving to meet the needs of a dynamic health system.

Who We Are

At PSN-YPG, we are the heart beat of the next generation of pharmacists in Nigeria. Our network is made up of young, dynamic professionals—pharmacists who are either 35 years or younger or have graduated from an accredited pharmacy school within the last five years. As the youth arm of the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN), we are committed to innovation, advocacy, and leadership in shaping the future of healthcare.

Our mission? To empower Young Pharmacists by equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and opportunities to lead, innovate, and transform pharmacy practice. Through policy advocacy, professional development programs, mentorship, and strategic partnerships, we ensure that Young Pharmacists have a voice in the evolving healthcare landscape. Whether you’re looking to connect, grow, or make an impact, PSN-YPG is your platform to engage, collaborate, and drive meaningful change in public health.
